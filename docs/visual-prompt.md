# 古时月 · 视觉总览图设计 Prompt

> 用途：交给设计 Agent（Midjourney / DALL-E / Stable Diffusion / 人类设计师）生成项目整体视觉总览图

---

## Prompt（英文，推荐用于 AI 图像生成）

```
A breathtaking isometric illustration of a floating traditional Chinese garden,
inspired by Monument Valley's impossible geometry aesthetic fused with Chinese
ink wash painting (水墨画) sensibility.

COMPOSITION:
- Isometric 45-degree top-down view, centered composition
- Seven floating stone platforms arranged in a star/constellation pattern around
  a central larger platform, connected by elegant stone bridges and spiraling
  staircases with impossible geometry (Penrose stairs, Escher-like space)
- Soft mist and clouds drifting between platforms, creating depth and dreamlike atmosphere

CENTRAL PLATFORM (focal point):
- A traditional Chinese pavilion (亭子) with curved upturned eaves (飞檐翘角),
  simplified low-poly geometric form
- A small character figure standing nearby: a tiny person wearing a cone-shaped
  hat and flowing robe, minimalist Monument Valley style, warm terracotta red robe
- Stone lanterns (石灯笼) glowing with warm amber light on either side
- A small circular pond reflecting the pavilion

SIX SURROUNDING PLATFORMS (each with distinct identity):
1. NORTH — Solar term terrace (节气台): a tall stone platform with a sundial (日晷)
   and carved stone tablet, surrounded by seasonal vegetation
2. NORTHWEST — Poetry stele forest (诗碑林): three to four carved stone tablets
   (石碑) arranged in a small grove, with faint glowing Chinese calligraphy
3. EAST — Vegetable & fruit garden (蔬果园): terraced garden plots with low-poly
   vegetables and fruit trees, small wooden fence
4. SOUTH — Flower garden (百花园): a field of low-poly flowers in bloom,
   varied colors representing seasonal flowers (花信风)
5. SOUTHWEST — Time clock plaza (时辰钟): a circular stone plaza with a water
   clock (水钟) or large bronze sundial, twelve hour markers carved on ground
6. SOUTHEAST — Ancient scholar's pavilion (古人亭): a smaller pavilion where a
   robed figure (ancient Chinese scholar NPC) stands waiting, with a glowing
   interaction ring on the ground

ARCHITECTURAL STYLE:
- All buildings use simplified low-poly geometry ( Monument Valley aesthetic )
- Chinese architectural elements: curved eaves, moon gates (月亮门),
  latticed windows (漏窗), stone arch bridges
- Clean flat-shaded surfaces, no textures, no realistic details
- Impossible geometry connections: stairs that loop, platforms at impossible angles

COLOR PALETTE (Chinese traditional colors + soft dreamy tones):
- Sky: gradient from dawn pink (霞色 #E8A4A0) to soft jade green (竹青 #A3C1A0)
  to muted gold (秋香色 #D9B96E), with ink wash (水墨) cloud texture
- Platform stone: warm grey (赭石 #8C6A4F) to muted beige (米色 #D4C5A0)
- Pavilion roofs: dark teal (黛色 #3D4B47) with gold accents (金 #C4A67A)
- Vegetation: seasonal greens (竹青 #A3C1A0), with seasonal accent colors
- Water: muted teal-blue (鸭蛋青 #B0C4C8)
- Character robe: warm terracotta (朱砂 #B8543E)
- Lanterns: warm amber glow (琥珀 #C4A67A)
- Overall mood: soft, dreamy, poetic, like a Chinese ink painting come to life

LIGHTING & ATMOSPHERE:
- Soft diffused lighting, like an overcast morning in a Chinese painting
- Warm amber glow from lanterns creating pools of light
- Subtle mist between platforms for depth
- Long soft shadows from a low-angle warm sun
- No harsh shadows, no dramatic contrast — gentle and contemplative

BACKGROUND:
- Distant misty mountains as simple silhouettes (远山如黛), ink wash style
- A large pale moon (古时月) visible in the sky, giving the project its name
- Floating particles like petals or fireflies drifting through the scene

ART STYLE REFERENCES:
- Monument Valley by ustwo games (geometry, isometric view, color blocking)
- Chinese ink wash painting (水墨画) for atmosphere and background
- Studio Ghibli backgrounds (soft, painterly, contemplative mood)
- Low-poly 3D art with flat shading
- Minimalist, clean, poetic, not cluttered

TECHNICAL:
- High detail, sharp clean lines
- Square aspect ratio (1:1) or slightly wide (4:3)
- No text, no UI elements, no characters other than the main figure and scholar NPC
- The overall feeling should be: serene, poetic, ancient Chinese wisdom meets
  modern game design elegance
```

## 中文说明（供人类设计师参考）

### 核心风格
纪念碑谷的等距几何美学 + 中国水墨园林意境。低多边形、平面着色，不要写实纹理。

### 构图要点
- 等距45度俯视角，居中构图
- 七个浮空石台呈七星拱月布局，中央主庭最大
- 石桥和旋转阶梯连接各平台，融入不可能几何（彭罗斯阶梯、埃舍尔空间）
- 平台间有云雾飘渺，营造仙境纵深

### 七大区域
| 位置 | 区域 | 标志物 |
|------|------|--------|
| 中央 | 主庭（角色起点） | 中式亭子 + 角色 + 石灯笼 + 水池 |
| 北 | 节气台 | 日晷 + 石碑 + 季节植被 |
| 西北 | 诗碑林 | 3-4块石碑，碑面隐约可见书法 |
| 东 | 蔬果园 | 阶梯菜畦 + 低多边形蔬果 + 木栅栏 |
| 南 | 百花园 | 低多边形花田，多色绽放 |
| 西南 | 时辰钟 | 圆形广场 + 水钟/铜日晷 + 十二时辰刻度 |
| 东南 | 古人亭 | 小亭子 + 古人NPC + 地面交互光圈 |

### 色彩体系（中国传统色）
- 天空：霞色 → 竹青 → 秋香色 渐变，带水墨云纹
- 石台：赭石 / 米色 暖灰系
- 屋顶：黛色 + 金色点缀
- 角色：朱砂红长袍
- 灯笼：琥珀色暖光
- 整体氛围：柔和、梦幻、诗意

### 光影氛围
- 柔和散射光，如水墨画中的清晨
- 灯笼散发琥珀色光晕
- 平台间有淡雾增加纵深
- 低角度暖阳产生柔和长影
- 不要强烈阴影或高对比

### 背景
- 远山如黛，水墨剪影
- 天空中一轮淡月（呼应"古时月"项目名）
- 飘落的花瓣或萤火虫粒子

### 风格参考
- 《纪念碑谷》ustwo games — 几何、等距、色块
- 中国水墨画 — 氛围与背景
- 吉卜力工作室背景 — 柔和、绘画感、宁静
- 低多边形3D艺术 + 平面着色

### 技术要求
- 高细节，线条干净利落
- 方形或4:3横向比例
- 无文字、无UI元素
- 除主角色和古人NPC外无其他人物
- 整体感受：宁静、诗意、古典智慧与现代游戏设计之美的融合

## 使用建议

### 如果用 Midjourney
在 prompt 末尾追加参数：
```
--ar 4:3 --s 750 --q 2 --v 6
```

### 如果用 DALL-E 3
直接使用英文 prompt，可补充一句：
```
The image should be in a 4:3 landscape format with high detail.
```

### 如果用 Stable Diffusion
建议追加负面提示词：
```
Negative prompt: realistic, photorealistic, texture, noisy, cluttered,
text, watermark, UI, hud, multiple characters, dark, gloomy, horror,
realistic lighting, high contrast
```

### 如果给人类设计师
直接提供中文说明部分 + 英文 prompt 作为参考，重点强调：
1. 纪念碑谷的几何简洁感（不要写实）
2. 中国传统色的柔和色调（不要饱和度过高）
3. 七大区域的布局关系和标志物
4. 水墨梦幻的整体氛围
